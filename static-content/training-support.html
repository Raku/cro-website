Training and Support

Commercial Support
Commercial support for Cro, together with the Rakudo Perl 6 compiler and the MoarVM virtual machine, is available from Edument AB. So if you run into a problem you just can't solve, you've got somebody to turn to. The Edument team created Cro, and so know its ins and outs. The team also contains a number of Perl 6 core contributors, so we can dive as deep down the stack as needed to solve a problem.
Training
Edument provides top-class education and mentoring. If you need a Cro or Perl 6 course, we can help. Those new to Perl 6 may find our Perl 6 for Programmers course valuable. We can also deliver courses on a wide range of other technologies and topics, such as JavaScript, React.js, and Git.
Feature bounties
Missing a feature in Cro? Need it now? Interested in funding its development? Get in contact!
General consulting
Fancy having the people behind Cro helping with your projects? It may well be possible! Learn more about what the Edument team can do.
